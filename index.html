Everything You Need to Manage Your Fitness Business

From client management to workout planning, we've got all the tools you need to deliver an exceptional fitness experience.

Client Management

Keep track of all your clients, their progress, and their fitness goals in one place. Send personalized messages and updates to keep them engaged.

Workout Planning

Create custom workout plans for each client based on their fitness level and goals. Include video demonstrations, sets, reps, and detailed instructions.

Nutrition Tracking

Develop personalized meal plans for your clients. Track macros, calories, and water intake to help them achieve their nutrition goals.

Progress Analytics

Track client progress with detailed analytics and visual reports. Monitor weight, measurements, strength gains, and more.

Scheduling

Manage your schedule and allow clients to book sessions directly through the platform. Automated reminders reduce no-shows.

Subscription Management

Set up and manage subscriptions for your training packages. Automated billing makes getting paid easy and reliable.
